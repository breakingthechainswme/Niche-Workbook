Phase 6 — Pre-Post Checklist
✅ Before You Hit Post
  • Hook is in the first 1–2 lines (no fluff before it)
  • Value is clear — viewer knows what they're getting
  • CTA is specific (not just "follow me")
  • Caption has at least 1–2 searchable keywords
  • 3–10 relevant hashtags added (not 30 random ones)
  • Visual is clean, on-brand, easy to read on mobile
  • Alt text added (accessibility + SEO boost)
  • Profile bio updated to reflect your niche
  • Profile photo is clear and represents your brand
  • You've checked the post previews in Instagram before publishing
  • You're posting at a good time for your audience timezone
  • You're ready to respond to comments in the first hour
